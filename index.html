I am a fourth-year undergraduate student at Bilkent University, Computer Science Department. I work under the supervision of Professor Yangfeng Ji in the UVA ILP Lab.
My research interest is natural language generation. In particular, I am interested in few-shot natural language generation, open-domian dialogue generation and evaluation, deep latent-variable models for stylistic text generation, and efficient learning algorithms for training deep generative neural networks.
